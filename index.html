Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at SRM University-AP specializing in predictive modeling and hybrid machine learning frameworks. I design robust and interpretable models for forecasting and diagnostic decision-making systems in finance and healthcare.

I completed my Ph.D. in CSE from Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan University, focusing on metaheuristic neural optimization combined with multi-criteria decision making.
