About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at SRM University-AP with a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan University (2025). My research focuses on integrating Machine Learning, Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), and Evolutionary Algorithms for financial time-series forecasting and medical image analysis.

I have developed novel hybrid models that reduce forecasting errors by up to 40% and published in SCIE/ESCI journals such as Cluster Computing and Applied Soft Computing.

Research Interests

  • Predictive Modeling & Financial Time-Series Analysis
  • Neural Architecture Optimization using Metaheuristics
  • AI in Healthcare: MRI-based Cancer Detection
  • Ensemble Learning with MCDM Weighting
  • Transfer Learning & Dimensionality Reduction
