Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at SRM University-AP, where I work in the areas of machine learning, predictive modeling, and hybrid optimization. My research focuses on developing reliable and interpretable computational models for complex real-world systems, especially within finance and healthcare.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University, where I specialized in neural architecture optimization and multi-criteria decision-making approaches for enhancing time-series forecasting accuracy. Prior to that, I worked in academic and research support roles, gaining hands-on experience in lab instruction, curriculum support, and student mentorship.

My work continues to explore the balance between algorithmic performance and meaningful model interpretability, aiming to create systems that are technically strong and practically applicable.
